football

rules of the game

  • stop when the whistle blows
  • obey the referee
  • play fairly

Archery

rules of the game

  • Always walk in archery ranges
  • keep arrow in quiver until you are told to do so
  • know and obey all range commands

Swimming

rules of the game

  • pulling on the lane rope is not allowed
  • the swimmer must stay and finish the race in the same lane in which he/she started.
  • all single races must be kept as seperate gender event

Javelin

rules of the game

  • The javelin must be thrown with an over the shoulder motion
  • athletes must chalk their hands
  • the competitor can't turn his back to the throwing area until the javelin is airborne

cycling

rules of the game

  • cyclists must not ride without holding the handlebars with at least one hand
  • they must not allow themselves to be towed by another vehicle
  • they must not pust objects which hamper their cycling or endanger other road users

Dogeball

rules of the game

  • NO head shots. You will be called out
  • NO holding a ball for longer than 10seconds
  • YOU ARE OUT IF:
    you are hit by a live ball
    you deflect a ball with a ball in your hands and drop it.
  • NO kicking a dodgeball.

Hockey

rules of the game

  • no slap shots (shots taken from above the waist) at all-not during warm-ups or games
  • no over-time during the regular season; tie games will be recorded as such
  • Games are 30 minute straight play.The clock will start at game time
